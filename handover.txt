SwitchRadar is a modernized lead intelligence platform designed for visual market analysis. It allows teams to import large datasets (JSON, XLSX, CSV), map them to a unified format, and visualize them on a high-performance interactive map with provider-specific branding.

- **Mapping**: Leaflet + React-Leaflet + MarkerCluster
- **Parsing**: SheetJS (XLSX)

## âœ¨ Key Features
1. **Visual Lead Intelligence**: Full-screen map view with diamond markers and advanced clustering.
2. **Branded Markers**: Custom icons for major providers (MTN, Telkom, Vodacom, VOX, etc.) with specific brand colors.
3. **Smart Import**:
   - Column-guessing algorithm.
   - Automatic Latitude/Longitude derivation from Google Maps Links.
   - Robust XLSX/JSON support.
4. **Data Persistence**: All imported leads stay in the browser (IndexedDB) until explicitly cleared.
5. **Dynamic Filtering**: Instant search and network layer toggles that sync between Map and Table views.

## âš¡ Performance Optimizations
- **Memoization**: Core filtering and map calculations are memoized to handle 2000+ leads without re-render lag.
- **Marker Clustering**: Automatically groups leads when zoomed out and scatters them (radius 40) for granular view.
- **IndexedDB**: Fast data access without hitting a backend.
